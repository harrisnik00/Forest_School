Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our forest school approach

  • What age groups do you accept?

    Our morning school program is designed for children aged 3-6 years. We also offer holiday camps for ages 4-10 years, and welcome potty-trained 2-year-olds on a case-by-case basis.

  • What happens in bad weather?

    We believe there's no such thing as bad weather, only inappropriate clothing! Our forest school operates year-round, and children learn to appreciate all seasons and weather conditions as part of their nature experience.

  • Are meals included in the program?

    Yes! Healthy meals based on the Mediterranean diet are included in all our full-day programs. We accommodate dietary requirements and allergies.

  • How do you ensure children's safety in the forest?

    Safety is our top priority. Our educators are trained in forest school safety protocols, first aid, and risk assessment. We maintain appropriate adult-to-child ratios and have comprehensive safety policies in place.
